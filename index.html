Arunachalam History

Arunachalam (Tiruvannamalai) is one of the most sacred places in India. It represents the 'Agni' (Fire) element of Lord Shiva. The hill itself is considered as the Shiva Lingam.

How to Reach?

🚌 Bus: Direct buses from Chennai & Bangalore.
🚆 Train: Tiruvannamalai Station (TNM).
🏍️ Bike: Scenic ride via Krishnagiri.
🏃 Walk: 14km Girivalam path.
